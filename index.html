How It Works

Plotana employs various types of satellite imagery—including optical images and night lights data— along with proprietary machine learning algorithms applied to this data, to derive following insights.

Development Score

The development score rates a location based on greenery, proximity to landmarks, development, economic activity, and flood risk.

Flood Risk

Flood risk is determined by the elevation of the area and its proximity to nearby water bodies.

Greenery

The level of greenery is determined by the percentage of land around a given location that is covered with trees.

Buildings

Building insights are based on nighttime illumination and the area’s closeness to various amenities.

Proximity

The proximity assessment is based on the distance of the location from key landmarks within the city.
